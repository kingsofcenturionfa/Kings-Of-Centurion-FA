ABOUT US

Kings of Centurion FA is a football club that believes that every child deserves to explore and improve in their skills.

We aim to develop skilled, confident and passionate players in a supportive, united and fun environment.

BENEFITS

Develop Teamwork & Social Skills

This is achieved by kids working together toward shared goals, building communication, friendship, respect, and sportsmanship.

Offer Physical Fitness

Kids keep active through running, jumping, and playing. This strengthens muscles, improves coordination, and boosts overall health.

Improve Soccer Skills

Through regular practice, coaching, and playing with others, kids learn passing, dribbling, shooting, and understanding game strategies.

Teach Discipline & Hard Work

This is achieved through regular practice, following rules, listening to coaches, and putting in consistent effort to improve performance.
